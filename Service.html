    Vehicle Acquisition.

    OUR Dealership purchase vehicles from manufacturers or wholesalers, often under franchise agreements. Sales. The primary function is to sell new and used vehicles to customers, often specializing in certain brands. Financing: Dealerships often offer financing options to make vehicle purchases more accessible, working with banks or financial institutions. Service and Maintenance: They provide maintenance and repair services, ensuring vehicles are well-maintained and reliable.
